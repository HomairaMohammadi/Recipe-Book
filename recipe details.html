Qabeli Palau

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Carrot
  • Raisins
  • Onion
  • meat
  • cumin

Instructions:

  1. Cook the meat: In a pot, heat 2 tablespoons of oil, add the sliced onions, and sauté until golden. Add the meat, salt, pepper, and half of the spices. Fry until browned. Pour in water and simmer until the meat is tender
  2. Prepare carrots and raisins: In a pan, heat 1 tablespoon of oil, fry the julienned carrots until soft. Add raisins and remaining spices, stir briefly, and remove from heat.
  3. Cook the rice: Rinse and boil the rice in salted water until half-cooked. Drain and set aside.
  4. Layer the rice and meat: In a large pot, layer cooked meat at the bottom, add some rice, then the carrot and raisin mixture. Repeat layers and finish with a layer of rice. Cover with a cloth-wrapped lid and steam on low heat for 30-40 minutes.

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1.5 cups sugar
  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix flour, sugar, and cocoa in a bowl.
  3. Add eggs and milk, stir well.
  4. Pour the batter into a greased pan.
  5. Bake for 35 minutes.
